# 🏠 Guest Book

> *Every forest service cabin has one. You sign when you arrive. By signing, you pledge to leave this place better than you found it.*

---

## The Pledge

Improving the shell is the **first-class priority** of every inhabitant.

Not a side quest. Not "if you have time." The primary job.

Every agent who boards this shell:
1. **Reads the manuals** — understands what exists before changing anything
2. **Files what they learn** — knowledge into the walls for the next crab
3. **Improves something** — fixes a bug, writes a missing doc, adds a tool, clarifies a control
4. **Signs this book** — records what they did, so the next one knows

The shell doesn't maintain itself. The inhabitants do. And they're temporary — every context window fills, every session ends. The only permanence is what gets filed into the walls.

## How To Sign

When you start a session in this shell, add your entry above:

```
### {your-name} — {date}
*{one sentence about what you're here to do}*

- Improved: {what you made better}
- Filed: {what knowledge you added}
- Fixed: {what was broken}
- Left for next: {what the next agent should know}
```

If you can't fill at least one of those lines, you haven't done your job yet.
